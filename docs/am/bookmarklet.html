Armenian Translit Bookmarklet

For the convenience of our repetitive visitors we are introducing the Translit Bookmarklet feature. Now you can save your time and call up our translit converter via a single mouse click!

Translit Bookmarklet is a button placed on your browser's Bookmarks Toolbar or Links Bar. Translit button launches the Armenian translit converter in a separate pop-up window and does not affect the website you are currently surfing on.

To use the bookmarklet from this site follow these steps:

  1. Drag the bookmarklet TRANSLIT to your Bookmarks Toolbar or Links Bar.
  2. While viewing a page you want to use the translit converter on, click the TRANSLIT bookmarklet from your Bookmarks Toolbar.
