Translit Webmaster Tools

Russian transliteration service helps many people all over the World to get a better access to the Russian language and script. Despite permanent improvement of the Translit service itself, we are constantly looking for an opportunity to support even more people in their day-to-day Internet experience. For this purpose we are encouraging webmasters and site owners to use our language services on their websites.

Add Translit to Your Site

If you own a website or a forum and would like to engage your visitors to write in Russian rather than in Russian Transliteration, then you are welcome to integrate our Russian Translit service into your site. To do so you simply need to insert a link (or a button), which will call up a pop-up window, allowing your visitors to type their message in Russian without even leaving your site. The typed text can be then copy-pasted by visitors into a text input form of your site.

Just copy the respective HTML code below and insert it somewhere near the text input form of your site.
